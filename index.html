Gwinnett
Bicentennial
Story Vault
During Gwinnett County’s Bicentennial Celebration, our Communications Division recorded Gwinnett residents sharing their stories about how we came to be and where we were in our 200th year. Stories from the past helped put the present in context and shaped a shared vision for the future. The stories came from people from all walks of life from members of Gwinnett’s founding families to newer residents of the county. We also produced a compilation of highlights for the closing ceremony.
